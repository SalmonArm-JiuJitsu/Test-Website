Enrollment Form

We're excited to have your child join us! Our goal is to create a positive, structured, and supportive environment where every student can grow, learn, and enjoy training. By registering for our youth program, you are agreeing to the following:

Terms

1. Season Commitment

Registration is for a full 4-month season. This helps us build consistency, develop skills, and create a strong team environment. By enrolling, you are committing to the full season, regardless of attendance or changes in your availability.

2. Attendance & Participation

Consistent attendance and active participation are key to your child's success, and the team's progress. We ask that all students:

  • Make their best effort to attend a minimum of three practices per week
  • Participate fully during training
  • Contribute positively as a teammate and training partner

We understand that occasional absences happen due to illness or exceptional circumstances, and we'll always be reasonable and supportive in those situations.

3. Team Culture & Parent Support

Jiu-jitsu can be challenging at times. This is a normal and important part of the growth process. We ask parents to support and encourage their child to work through those challenges, rather than stepping away.


If any concerns come up with participation or behavior, we'll communicate and work together to support your child so they, and the team, have the best possible experience.

4. Payment

The total program fee is $595 + GST for the 4-month season. Full payment is required at the time of registration to secure your child's spot. Spaces are limited and are filled on a first-come, first-served basis.

5. Refunds & Withdrawals

All registrations are final. As your child's spot is reserved for the full season, we do not offer refunds, credits, or transfers for missed classes, withdrawals, schedule conflicts, or injury or illness.


This allows us to keep classes consistent and plan staffing and programming properly.


If your child is unable to train due to injury or illness, we will support a safe return to training when they're ready.

6. Health & Hygiene

To help prevent skin infections and keep everyone healthy:

  • Check your child regularly for skin issues (redness, irritation, itchiness, bumps, or ring-shaped marks)
  • Ensure they shower immediately after class
  • Send them in clean training clothes each practice
  • Cover any cuts or scrapes before class
  • Ensure their nails are trimmed short prior to training
